# Mechanics

The Water Bucket can collect water from any procedurally generated water sources.
To fill the bucket up hold the Right Mouse Button. The bucket is filled to its maximum capacity in less than a second.
To empty the Water Bucket press the Left Mouse Button. The contents of the bucket will be chucked in the direction you were originally aiming at.
Water, hurled with a bucket, behaves like a sluggish, short-ranged, parabolic projectile with a maximum range of 5 m.
Water, splashed this way, is able to extinguish Camp Fires, regular Furnaces, Large Furnaces, Lanterns, fires generated by Flame Throwers, Incendiary Rockets and Incendiary or Explosive ammunition. Splashed water may also be absorbed by nearby Water Barrels.
Once filled, switching to a different item on your Action Bar, deselecting or placing the filled bucket in your Inventory, will cause its contents to immediately spill on the ground.
